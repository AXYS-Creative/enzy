Leaderboard

Experience the power of a highly customizable leaderboard tailored to your unique sales needs. Choose from multiple views to gain valuable insights into your company's performance, from the big-picture snapshot to specific deal details. Monitor sales reps, regions, and date sections, ensuring you have the most up-to-date and comprehensive information to drive your sales success.

Messaging

Elevate team communication with our advanced messaging feature. Equipped with rights protection, polls, events, and multimedia support, our messaging tool is designed to keep your team connected and engaged. Foster a collaborative environment that meets and exceeds industry standards for seamless communication.

Incentives

Motivate and inspire your sales force with the innovative incentives feature. Regionals and team leads can create engaging tournaments and milestones that drive performance and foster a healthy competitive spirit. Encourage your team to excel by recognizing their hard work and achievements.

Badges

Celebrate your sales team's accomplishments with our dynamic badge system. Recognize and reward milestones that align with your company's values and goals, promoting a sense of pride and achievement among your sales force.

Reports

Stay informed and agile with our automatically generated reports, offering crucial insights into your company's health. Make data-driven decisions and strategize effectively, ensuring the continued growth and success of your sales team.
